## Ingredients

* 8 cups flour
* 2 stick butter, melted
* 2 cups of milk
* 4 cups of sugar
* 1 1/2 tsp mastica
* 2 tsp mahlab
* 1 tbsp mazaner
* 1 tbsp maward
* 1 tbsp baking powder
* 1 tbsp yeast
* 1/2 tbsp ground nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients together.
2. Let it rest for about an hour.
3. Shape into circles then bake.

Enjoy!
