## Ingredients

* 18 cloves of garlic
* 1 tbsp salt
* 1/2 lemon, juiced
* 1 cup veg. oil
* 2 tbsp corn starch
* 1 cup and a bit of water

## Directions

1. Boil corn starch and water together.
2. Blend or process everything together.

Enjoy!
