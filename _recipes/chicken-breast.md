## Ingredients

* 3 tbsp oil
* 4 lbs chicken
* salt and peper for taste
* 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* 1/2 tsp onion powder
* 1/2 tsp dried basil
* 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
* 2 tbsp butter

Enjoy!
