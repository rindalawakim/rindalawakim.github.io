## Ingredients

* 1 tbsp salt
* 3 tsp Italian herb
* 2 tsp smoked paprika
* 2 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tsp dry ground mustard
* 1 tsp brown sugar
* 1/2 tsp onion powder
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Enjoy!
