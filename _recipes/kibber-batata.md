## Ingredients

* 2 big potatoes
* 1 medium onion
* 1/4 cup bulgar + 1/2 cup hot water
* 1 tbsp bulgar
* 2 tsp salt
* some mint
* 1 tsp kibbeh spice

Enjoy!
